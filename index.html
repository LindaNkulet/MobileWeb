Ashesi University College

Welcome to the official website of Ashesi University College.Ashesi University is a private,non-profit,liberal arts college in Ghana,West Africa.It was established in 2002 by Patrick Awuah Jr.

It offers a four-year bachelors programme featuring the following majors.

  • Business Administration
  • Computer Science
  • Management Information Systems
  • Electrical and Electronic Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering Engineering
